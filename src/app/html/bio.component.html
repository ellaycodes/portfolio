bio

Professor Kemi Yekini is a professor of Accounting at Aston Business School, Aston University. She holds a PhD in Accounting and Finance, an MSc in Finance, and a BSc in Accounting. As a chartered accountant (ICAN) and certified fraud examiner (CFE), she has contributed significantly to academic research in her field, publishing extensively in highly-ranked international journals. Her academic career spans roles such as Director of Doctoral Studies and Programme Director. She is recognized for her sustainability, governance, and corporate social responsibility research leadership.
